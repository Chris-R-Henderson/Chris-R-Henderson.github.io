This is a thrust vector proof of concept project that was designed to attempt to achieve hovering flight using a gimble and monopropeller. This project came from a desire to design a thrust vectoring system for the CSULB Beach Launch Team rocket. The main focus of the project was to figure out a flight controller that would work in conjunction with two accelerometers to detect the changing center of gravity as the rocket burns its propellant. Center of gravity on a rocket is an important variable in solving the problem of directing thrust as needed to stabilize the rocket in hovering flight. Ultimately this project will be adapted to the Pubworks engine as a next step. I worked on this project with Brandon Cavanaugh and I would like to acknowledge his leading roll in the developement of this system.
